Visit Us

Troop meetings are usually held on the 2nd and 4th Wednesday of every month at 7:00 PM in the basement of Des Peres City Hall. Go to the calendar to see the schedule.

It will be great to meet you and answer any questions you have about Troop 313 or Boy Scouts in general. Hope to see you soon!

Let us know if you are coming by emailing Scoutmaster Jim Riddle at scoutmaster@troop313.org so that we can have a packet of information ready for you.

How to join?

You must be a boy who has completed the fifth grade or is 11 years old, or has earned the Arrow of Light Award, but is under 18 years old. Everyone is welcome.
